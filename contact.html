Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the weather data?

Our weather data is sourced from OpenWeatherMap API, which provides accurate real-time weather information. The data is typically updated hourly and is as accurate as meteorological forecasting allows.

Is the Country Weather App free to use?

Yes! The TNT Dynamix Country Weather App is completely free to use. We believe weather information should be accessible to everyone without any cost barriers.

How do I save my favorite countries?

When viewing a country's weather information, simply click on the "Add to Favorites" button. Your favorites are saved locally on your device and can be accessed quickly for future reference.

Can I use this app on my mobile device?

Absolutely! Our app is built with responsive design principles, ensuring it works beautifully on desktop computers, tablets, and mobile phones.

How can I request a new feature?

We love hearing new ideas! Please use our contact form above and select "Feature Feedback" as the subject. Our team reviews all suggestions when planning future updates.
