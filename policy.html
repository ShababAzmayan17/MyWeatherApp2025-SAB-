Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to TNT Dynamix Country Weather App. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

The Data We Collect

Our weather application collects and processes the following data:

  • Search Queries: We store the countries you search for to provide the "Recent Searches" feature.
  • Favorite Countries: We store your favorite countries to enhance your user experience.
  • Theme Preferences: We save your light/dark mode preference.
  • Device Information: We collect basic device information to optimize our service for your device.

All this data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage and is not transmitted to our servers unless explicitly needed for providing the weather service.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To personalize your experience
  • To improve our website and services
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support

Third-Party APIs

Our application uses the following third-party APIs:

  • RestCountries API: Used to fetch country information.
  • OpenWeatherMap API: Used to fetch weather data for the capital cities.

When you search for a country, your search query is shared with these services. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for information on how they handle your data.

Data Retention

We retain your locally stored data until you clear your browser's localStorage or you manually remove items from your recent searches or favorites.

Your Data Protection Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: You can access all your data in localStorage through your browser's developer tools.
  • Correction: You can update your preferences at any time.
  • Erasure: You can clear your data by clearing your browser's localStorage.

Cookie Policy

Our website does not use cookies directly, but relies on localStorage to save your preferences and recent searches. Third-party APIs we use may employ their own cookies according to their respective policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact Page.
